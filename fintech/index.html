Case Study · Fintech

A Multi-Brand Salesforce Underwriting Platform

Custom Salesforce development for a multi-brand financial services underwriting platform, advanced Lightning Web Components, credit data integrations, and embedded decision-support logic.

Financial Services · Multi-Brand Lender · Salesforce Sales Cloud

Executive Summary

Free Thinkers Consulting partnered with a rapidly expanding financial services organization to support the buildout of a brand-new Salesforce underwriting org serving multiple lending brands. While the client's internal Salesforce administrators led core configuration and foundational setup, Free Thinkers Consulting operated as the custom development arm, engineering advanced Lightning Web Components, credit data integrations, and embedded decision-support logic that powered mission-critical underwriting workflows. The result was a scalable, custom-built underwriting platform combining strong administrative governance with advanced Salesforce engineering.

Business Context

The client operates a family of small business financing brands, each requiring structured underwriting processes to evaluate credit data, bank statement performance, multi-guarantor applications, loan eligibility thresholds, and funding recommendations.

The organization chose Salesforce as the foundation for a new underwriting system. Internal administrators were responsible for core object configuration, standard Salesforce setup, and basic workflow implementation. However, the underwriting process required advanced custom development beyond declarative capabilities, including:

  • Complex financial data presentation
  • Real-time credit integrations
  • Embedded calculation logic
  • Custom user interfaces
  • Sensitive data permission modeling

Free Thinkers Consulting was engaged as the dedicated development partner to build this advanced engineering layer.

Technical Challenges

1. Financial Data Usability

Raw bank statement data and credit information were difficult to analyze using standard Salesforce layouts. Underwriters needed structured, interactive views to evaluate borrower performance efficiently.

2. Inconsistent or Incomplete Financial Inputs

Bank data frequently required normalization, correction, or structured formatting before underwriting decisions could be made.

3. Multi-Guarantor Complexity

Applications often involved multiple guarantors with varying financial relationships and risk profiles.

4. Sensitive Underwriting Permissions

Underwriting data contained highly sensitive financial information requiring granular access control across roles.

5. Cross-Brand Scalability

The platform needed to support multiple lending brands and Salesforce instances without requiring duplicated engineering effort.

Solution Architecture

Free Thinkers Consulting provided custom Salesforce engineering focused on building the advanced application, UX/UI, and integration layer powering underwriting operations.

1. Advanced Lightning Web Components for Underwriting Workflows

We designed and implemented multiple custom Lightning Web Components (LWCs) to transform raw underwriting data into structured, decision-ready interfaces. Key components included:

  • Bank statement visualization dashboards
  • Inline-editable financial line items for correcting incomplete or inconsistent data
  • Structured multi-guarantor data handling
  • Credit review summary interface
  • CLEAR (Comprehensive Loan Evaluation & Analysis Review) page
  • Application status visualization component for non-technical stakeholders

These LWCs provided real-time operational clarity for credit analysts and sales teams operating inside Salesforce daily.

2. Credit Pull & Financial Data Integrations

We engineered custom integrations supporting credit data ingestion, external financial data synchronization, real-time mapping into Salesforce records, and structured normalization of external financial inputs. By centralizing financial intelligence inside Salesforce, underwriting teams reduced reliance on disconnected tools and manual processes.

3. Embedded Lending Logic & Decision Support

To assist underwriting consistency, we implemented custom calculation logic supporting borrower financial strength evaluation, funding amount recommendations, lending threshold assessment, and structured scoring outputs. This reduced manual computation cycles and improved consistency across underwriting decisions.

4. Role, Profile & Permission Architecture

Given the sensitivity of financial and credit data, we implemented granular role hierarchies, profile-based access segmentation, component-level visibility controls, and secure data separation across brands. This ensured compliance integrity while maintaining operational efficiency.

5. Multi-Brand & Multi-Instance Support

The organization operates across multiple lending brands and Salesforce environments. Our custom development approach ensured reusable Lightning Web Components, brand-level configuration flexibility, scalable deployment patterns, and sustainable long-term enhancement under managed services. As the family of brands expands, the underwriting platform remains stable and extensible.

Operational Impact

The centralized Salesforce underwriting org delivered measurable operational improvements:

  • 60% faster underwriting turnaround time
  • Standardized decision-making criteria across subsidiaries
  • Reduced operational overhead through automation
  • Eliminated redundant manual review roles
  • Increased visibility into underwriting pipeline metrics
  • Enabled scalable acquisition integration

Most importantly, the client transitioned from a resource-heavy, manual underwriting model to a scalable, automation-driven system aligned with long-term growth.

Ongoing Managed Services

Following the initial development engagement, Free Thinkers Consulting continues to provide managed services support, including enhancement development, LWC refinements, integration maintenance, architecture evolution, and support for new lending brand expansion.
